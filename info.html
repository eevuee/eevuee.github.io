Info

Hi! My name is usernamehere.

I'm a student, freelance artist and animator as well as a streamer and a generally online person. I started drawing as a kid but have recently started taking it a bit more seriously. I currently have very little free time, but the time I have is spent by reading, crocheting and sewing, and playing games. During these past couple of years, I've worked with multiple individuals and companies to bring their ideas to life; be it 2D or 3D. My creative hobbies help me take my mind off of the stress and I feel very privileged to be able to make a living from doing something I love! In the future, I am to graduate from my current studies and gain more tech-related knowledge to further my somewhat unserious life goal of becoming a 21st century Renaissance man.
